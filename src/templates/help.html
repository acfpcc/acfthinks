ACF THINKS

Welcome!

Thanks for installing ACF Thinks! ACF Thinks blocks Facebook and Youtube until you have jotted down your thought for the day on the topic or passage that week.

What does ACF Thinks do?

When you load Facebook or Youtube, ACF Thinks pops up and encourages you to write down a thought for that day. After you have entered in your thought, ACF Thinks fades away and you can carry on using Facebook and Youtube as per usual.

How do I enter in a thought?

To enter in your thought, open up Facebook or Youtube, and the pop up will appear. Type your thought in the grey box and press Enter to store your thought. To add new lines in the grey box, press Shift+Enter.

How do I see my previous thoughts?

To see your previous thoughts, click on the icon in the main Google Chrome toolbar on the top righthand side.

How do I delete previous thoughts?

To delete your previous thoughts, click on the icon in the main Google Chrome toolbar on the top righthand side. You can then delete each thought by clicking the button.

How do I delete all my data?

To delete all your stored data, click on the icon in the main Google Chrome toolbar on the top righthand side. You can then clear all your data by clicking the button.
